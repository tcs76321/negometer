Neg-O-Meter

Tracking the Sources of Negativity


The media, in tandem with other institutions, is highly incentivized to induce various degrees of panic in their viewers, and by extension society overall. Sensational news encourages viewers to return each day, or even each hour, to get updates on exactly how and where a part of the world is burning. This amplification of darkness in the world is incredibly damaging to our civilization.

As with any issue, calm awareness is the first step forward. Negometer attempts to analyze, rate, and report upon the negativity of various major news networks. The website is currently in its earliest stages of conception and realization. Over time, with work, it will grow and be refined to provide detailed daily reports and highly informative statistics which will inform on the degree of morbidity, despair, and hate of different media outlets. Hopefully, this will enable various individuals comprising the public to resist an induction of hysteria.
