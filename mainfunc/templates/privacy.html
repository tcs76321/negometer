Privacy and Data Collection Policy

This is the privacy policy for negometer.com



Negometer.com utilizes various third-party services to function. In doing so, 'cookies' and other tracking mechanisms are embedded into the website. Information collected includes but is not limited to IP address, website engagement, advertising related information, and website preferences.

This policy may be changed in the future and will be updated here.


If you have questions or concerns please contact us.


We value your security and privacy and utilize top of the line technology and web hosting to provide you our services. However, we will point out to you that no form of transmission over the internet is absolutely secure.
